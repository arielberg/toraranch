Halutz Program

About Us

As we all know, Am Yisrael and the State of Israel are facing major challenges today. The Halutz program has chosen to rise from today’s crises and embark upon a new endeavor to cultivate the Jewish leaders of tomorrow; those who will strengthen our Nation and our presence in the Land, and become the "pioneers" of tomorrow in all aspects of life.

Our Vision

The Halutz program prepares its participants for the challenges of adult Jewish life today, creating a new cadre of ‘pioneers’ that will see themselves leading our People in various fields, based on in-depth Torah understandings and a living connection to the Land of Israel developed by actually working the land.

How Does It Work?

Halutz participants will participate as pioneers building our homeland, engaging intimately with those most passionately committed to the Jewish people's story. The daily schedule includes study hours in the Beit Midrash, where participants clarify Torah and Jewish faith and apply insights with mentors to meet the challenges of the day.

Leading Values

  • Freedom - Room for choice and independence.
  • Responsibility - Active role in day-to-day work and management.
  • Trust - Mutual trust between staff and participants.

Location

The Halutz program is located on the Pnei Kedem Farm, southeast Gush Etzion, on the edge of the Judean desert. Participants live, study, and work on the farm while developing the Land.

Program

What is it?

A program that affords young adults the opportunity to participate as pioneers in the building of our homeland, while engaging intimately with those Israelis most passionately committed to the living story of the Jewish People.

How long is it?

Participants stay from three months to a year. No shorter than a two-month stay is recommended.

Cost

$1400 per month for room and board. Scholarships may be available.

Workshops

  • Stone-masonry, carpentry, metal-work
  • Vineyards, grains, vegetable gardens
  • Sheep farming
  • Geo-politics, history, music
  • Torah studies

FAQs
